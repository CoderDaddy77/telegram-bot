About Vera Media Tools

What is Vera Media Tools?

Vera Media Tools is a free, open-source all-in-one media toolkit built for speed and simplicity. Download videos, extract audio, convert files, and resize images — no accounts or signups required.

How does it work?

Powered by yt-dlp for video downloading and FFmpeg for media processing. Image resizing runs entirely in your browser using the Canvas API — no upload needed. Everything else runs server-side — your browser just sends requests and receives the final files.

Available tools

  • Video Downloader — Download MP4 videos up to 4K from 1000+ sites.
  • Video to MP3 — Extract high-quality MP3 audio from any video URL.
  • WAV → MP3 Converter — Convert WAV files to MP3 at your chosen bitrate.
  • Video to GIF — Convert video clips into animated GIFs. Coming soon.
  • Image Compressor — Compress PNG/JPEG without quality loss. Coming soon.

Is it free?

Yes — 100% free, no ads, no tracking, no hidden costs.
