About V Media Tools

What is V Media Tools?

V Media Tools is a free, open-source media toolkit built for speed and privacy. Convert audio, compress images and videos — no accounts, no signups, no data uploaded to any server.

How does it work?

Everything runs entirely in your browser using WebAssembly and the Canvas API. Your files never leave your device — no cloud processing, no server uploads.

Image Compressor uses the Canvas API with binary search to hit your target size.
WAV → MP3 and Video to MP3 use lamejs / FFmpeg.wasm for in-browser encoding.
Video Compressor uses FFmpeg.wasm (H.264) to process video locally.

Available tools

  • Video to MP3 — Upload any video, extract MP3 audio in your browser.
  • WAV → MP3 Converter — Convert WAV files to MP3 at your chosen bitrate.
  • Image Compressor — Compress PNG/JPEG/WebP to a target size in KB.
  • Video Compressor — Reduce video file size with target MB, %, or CRF control.
  • Image Converter — Convert between PNG, JPEG, WebP and BMP formats.
  • Images to PDF — Combine multiple images into a single PDF document.
  • Word to PDF — Convert DOCX documents to PDF in your browser.
  • PPT to PDF — Convert PowerPoint presentations to PDF.

Is it free?

Yes — 100% free, no ads, no tracking, no hidden costs. Your files are processed locally and never stored anywhere.
